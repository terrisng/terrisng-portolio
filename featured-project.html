This is one of the website designs I have worked on in CCT460 - Advanced Web Design using WordPress.org, along with custom HTML, CSS, JavaScript.

This particular website has a total of eight pages: (Click on bold text to expand for more information)

  1. Homepage

    2. The homepage includes navigation across all inner pages, in both navigation panel and elements on the page (i.e. banners)

    Some elements from inner pages are also highlighted

    • A carousel that features the product that the company is offering
    • A banner in red that invite users to check out the about us page
    • A section that features recent posts from the blog page of the site
    • A banner in yellow that link users to contact us page
    • A gallery section with photos
    • A newsletter signup section for users to register their email to receive latest information from us
    • A banner in red that invite users to check out the biography page
  2. About us

    3. The about us page features information about the startup company

    The about us page first introduce the background information of the company and products that we offer to customers

    After that, for the purpose of clarification, the second section indicates that currently there is no physical store while stating the location of where the shop is located / where products will be shipped from

    Lastly, there is a FAQ section where it attempts to answer some of the frequently asked questions from customers

  3. Biography

    4. The biography page features information about myself

    The overall design of the biography page took a different approach in terms of its theme and other design aspects for the purpose of differentiating it from the rest of the web pages on this website due to its topic being less related to the overall website.

    Header: Since the header is one of the first things that users will notice when they land on any website, I decided to change it on the biography page to help differentiate this page from other web pages

    Background: Since this is a biography page with information about myself, I decided to take a less formal approach in designing the page – via using a more cartoon theme background image that matches the theme of the website

  4. Blog

    5. The Blog page features blog posts that are updated frequently

    A simple web page that displays blog posts released by my company

    Design: Remains the overall theme of the whole website

    Functionality: Display blog posts according to the newest releases

  5. Contact us

    6. The contact us page allow users to send message to me using the embedded form

    The contact us uses a simple design that includes an embedded form which allow users to send messages, comments, or event business inquires to us using the contact form

    Design: Simple design that attempts to avoid or eliminate any confusion

    Functionality: A web form that allows users to send messages

  6. Shop

    7. The shop page list out all of products that the company is offering

    Design: Keeps the overall design theme of the website to ensure no confusion for customers (that they are still on the same website)

    Functionality: Displays products that are sold by our company, allow customers to sort product based on their product series, click on each individual products’ image to link themselves to the product page, and click on the “add to cart” button to add the product to their check-out chart

  7. Product

    8. The product page features the details of an product

    This is the inner page that pops up after users click on any products

    Functionality: This page displays information about products while allowing users to add the product to the cart after viewing the details of the product

  8. Event Registration

    9. The event registration page allow users to register into events using the embedded form

    Functionality: Allow users to register in events through an online form instead of having to email their details to us. Under the registration form, there is also a map showing the location where our pop-up store will be in

Along with some inner pages such as blog posts, check-out pages, etc...
